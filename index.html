About MAAC Animation

MAAC animation institute in Delhi offers a wide range of career-oriented courses in 3D Animation, VFX, Multimedia, Graphic & Web Designing, Gaming, & more. Work with software like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere, Autodesk 3dsmax, Autodesk Maya, Autodesk Mudbox, Eyeon Fusion, Apple Final Cut Pro, Autodesk Matchmover & more.

MAAC has a dedicated Research & Development team consisting of industry professionals. This team is responsible for planning the detailed curriculum for each course.MAAC Animation conducts faculty training programs to ensure high standards of teaching in the classroom. All teachers at MAAC Animation are trained by Animation industry professionals who help sharpen their creative & technical skills.

MAAC Animation institute in Delhi lays strong emphasis on using the best infrastructure to train students. MAAC Animation uses high-end computers, Wacom tablets & other equipment in the classroom. The infrastructure is similar to that used in the global Animation & VFX industry. This helps create an 'on-the-job' environment in the class. As a result, MAAC students often win many Awards for the films they create.

Maya Academy Of Advanced Cinematics has put a sincere attempt on part of a few committed professionals in the field of Animation & Digital Art Media. It is our endeavor to turn out self-driven, top class professionals in this field who derive utmost satisfaction from challenges at work and deadlines. This industry focus sets us apart from others in this field and if, we may say so, is also one of our competitive advantages.

Apart from Academics, MAAC Animation hosts several events to develop the skills of the students. The 24FPS International Animation Awards is among the most well-known events in the Animation industry. This event is a unique platform for film-making using the medium of 3D Animation, Live Action and Visual Effects (VFX). The competition for the Awards is open to students, professionals and studios from across the world.Through different events like 24FPS, National Students Meet, Srijan, Legends of MAAC and Next in Bollywood, MAAC Animation students get an opportunity to showcase their skills & to interact with experienced professionals. MAAC is the first and only Animation Academy that has a dedicated team that works all year round to organize events, which of course results in outstanding job placements.At MAAC Animation , each day is filled with learning & creating something new. That is why the students work gets selected for major festivals & wins awards in India and overseas. MAAC has strong affiliations with some of the leading organizations in this industry & Its Placements Cell has successfully placed candidates in companies like RHYTHM & HUES, RED CHILLIES,PRIME FOCUS, PIXION etc.

Events

MAAC Janakpuri hosts several events every year where students get an opportunity to showcase their skills & to interact with experienced Animation, VFX and Gaming professionals. Get the latest information on popular events like National Students' Meet, Srijan & Legends of MAAC, below. MAAC is the first - and only - Animation Academy that has a dedicated Team that works all year round to organize events. These events provide an opportunity for students to showcase their skills & to interact with experienced professionals from the Animation, VFX and Gaming industry. The education gained from these events help students become job-ready by the time they complete their course.

24 FPS

Held every year, the 24FPS International Animation Awards are a unique platform for film-making using the medium of 3D Animation, Live Action and Visual Effects (VFX). The Awards are open to students, professionals and studios from across the world

NSM

MAAC students get an opportunity to meet & interact with industry professionals to learn about exclusive developments, methods & achievements of the animation, film-making & VFX industry. Students participate in various teambuilding exercises & gain insights into the working environment in Animation studios.

Darpan

MAAC Janakpuri hosts students work exhibition "DARPAN" every year where students show their skills.These events provide an opportunity for students to showcase their skills & to interact with experienced professionals from the Animation, VFX and Gaming industry. The education gained from these events help students become job-ready by the time they complete their course.

MCL

MAAC's Srijan Edufair is a well-known event in the industry. The Edufair showcases the best work created by MAAC students in the categories of: Pre-ProductionProductionPost-Production During the Edufair, industry professionals showcase animation projects and conduct live contests, workshops, seminars & case studies. They also mentor & guide students on various industry trends & software/ technologies currently in use.
